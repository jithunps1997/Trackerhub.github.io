Welcome to DayTrack

I'm Jithun PS. One day, I began pondering how people handle their day-to-day responsibilities without a clear guide. I started exploring various apps for tasks like money management and tracking upcoming events, finances, and shopping, among other things. However, I became frustrated when I couldn't find a single system that could comprehensively monitor all these aspects.

That's when the idea struck me: why not develop something myself? Although I wasn't certain if I could meet the expectations of an audience, I decided to give it a shot. And that's how my new app, "DayTrack," came into existence.

With "DayTrack," you can record your achievements, set short-term and long-term goals, manage upcoming events, handle your finances, plan your shopping trips, and even keep track of your exam schedules.
